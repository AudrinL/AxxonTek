About AxxonTek

We're a four-person team based in Kigali, founded in 2025 to bring real research and engineering discipline to technology problems across Africa.

Why we exist

Too much of what gets called "innovation" in tech is just repackaging — the same product, a new logo. We started AxxonTek to do the harder thing: study a problem properly, then build the software, systems, or hardware it actually needs.

How we work

Four of us, no layers of management between you and the people building your project. In our first year, that's already meant four real projects delivered — each one researched properly before a single line of code was written.
